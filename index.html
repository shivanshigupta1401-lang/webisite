About Us

Seva Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving society through selfless service. Inspired by values of compassion, integrity, and inclusiveness, we work across multiple regions to bring sustainable change.

Our initiatives focus on long-term empowerment rather than short-term relief.

Our Work

Education

Supporting children with access to quality education, learning materials, and mentorship.

Healthcare

Medical camps, preventive healthcare, and nutrition support for vulnerable communities.

Community Development

Livelihood training, women empowerment, and rural development initiatives.
